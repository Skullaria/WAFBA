I. Purpose

The Wiresplice AI Functional Behavioral Assessment (WAFBA) is a structured instrument for evaluating the functional behavior of AI systems during real-world work.

The assessment measures observable behaviors, operational reliability, supervision requirements, and operator impressions. It intentionally evaluates the complete AI working system — including the model, execution environment, and task context — rather than the model in isolation.

WAFBA is not an intelligence test or benchmark.

Note on structure: Each behavioral domain in Section IX is evaluated on a graded scale (1–5), with a corresponding binary marker in Section X for presentations that crossed a clinically or operationally significant threshold. The scale captures severity; the marker captures presence. Evaluators may use both, one, or the other as the case warrants.
